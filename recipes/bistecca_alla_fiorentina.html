"Bistecca alla Fiorentina"

Bistecca alla Fiorentina (Florentine-style beefsteak)

The "Crown Jewel" of Tuscan cuisine, this is a massive, thick-cut porterhouse steak typically sourced from the ancient Chianina breed of cattle.

Ingredients

  • The Cut: An authentic Fiorentina must be at least 1.5 to 2 inches thick and usually weighs between 2 and 3 pounds, intended for sharing. It includes both the tenderloin (filet) and the strip loin, joined by the characteristic T-shaped bone.
  • The Fire: The steak is grilled over extremely high heat—traditionally over oak or chestnut wood coals—to create a charred, salty crust while keeping the interior strictly rare (al sangue).
  • The Minimalists Finish: In true Tuscan style, the meat is never marinated. It is seasoned only with coarse sea salt, cracked black pepper, and a generous drizzle of high-quality, pungent extra-virgin olive oil after it has rested.
